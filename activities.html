What We Do

Club Activities

Building language, leadership, and literary skills together

🗣️

Spoken English Practice

Regular interactive sessions designed to build fluency, confidence, and everyday communication skills in English for students at all levels.

  • Weekly practice sessions
  • Group conversations & role plays
  • Pronunciation workshops
  • Peer feedback & coaching
🏆

Competitions

Exciting and intellectually stimulating competitions that sharpen critical thinking, argumentation, and creative expression.

  • Inter-department debates
  • English quiz contests
  • Essay writing competitions
  • Public speaking events
📖

Book Review

Guided reading and analysis sessions that deepen literary understanding, build interpretive skills, and foster a genuine love for literature.

  • Monthly book selections
  • Group discussion sessions
  • Written review workshops
  • Author & context studies
📰

Magazine Publication

A student-led magazine that showcases the creative and academic talent of our members — from poetry and short stories to essays and club news.

  • Creative writing submissions
  • Poetry & short fiction
  • Academic essays
  • Club event coverage
💼

Career Development

Open and informative discussions on career pathways, professional opportunities, and growth strategies for English Department students.

  • Career pathway seminars
  • CV & interview workshops
  • Alumni talks & mentoring
  • Industry exposure sessions
💬

Topic-Wise Discussion

Structured and engaging conversations on literature, language, culture, and contemporary issues to broaden perspective and analytical thinking.

  • Literary theme discussions
  • Language & linguistics talks
  • Contemporary issue debates
  • Cultural exchange sessions
