How to Add Photos to This Gallery

01

Upload your photo files (JPG or PNG) to your GitHub repository in a folder called images/

02

In this file, find a gallery-item div and replace the placeholder with: <img src="images/yourphoto.jpg" alt="Event name">

03

Update the caption text inside the .caption div to describe the photo.

04

Commit and push to GitHub. Your gallery updates automatically within minutes.
