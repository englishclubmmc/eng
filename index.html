What We Do

Club Activities

🗣️

Spoken English Practice

Regular sessions to build fluency, confidence, and everyday communication skills in English.

🏆

Competitions

Debate, quiz contests, and essay writing events to sharpen critical thinking and expression.

📖

Book Review

Guided reading and analysis sessions that deepen literary understanding and interpretive skills.

📰

Magazine Publication

A student-led magazine showcasing creative writing, essays, poetry, and club news.

💼

Career Development

Open discussions on career pathways, opportunities, and professional growth for students.

💬

Topic-Wise Discussion

Structured conversations on literature, language, culture, and contemporary issues.
